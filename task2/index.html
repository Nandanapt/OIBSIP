About Muniba

Muniba Mazari is a Pakistani activist, motivational speaker, artist, model, singer, and television host. Despite being bound to a wheelchair after a tragic car accident at the age of 21, she has risen as a symbol of strength and resilience. Her journey is a powerful testament to the strength of the human spirit.

She became Pakistan’s first wheelchair-using model and was named in the BBC 100 Women list. Her artwork, often vibrant and emotional, is another outlet through which she expresses her inner world and encourages others to never give up.

Muniba continues to inspire millions around the world with her message: "You are the hero of your own life story."
